C&B Bakery and Supermarket

C&B Bakery and Supermarket is a Basotho owned business aiming to fulfil all the Basotho needs everytime.It was founded on the 26 of July 2019 by Mr Katleho Joshua Tsehlo and hid other two partners.They had a dream of reducing unemployment in Lesotho so they decided to raise capital and start their own business so that is how this company began.

When the company opened they hired only 10 employees in the Bakery and 15 inside the Supermarket,but ever since then the company has been running very good.The profits were high and that really showed progress inside the business, and the progress resulted in to more job opportunities.They hired extra 10 employees in the Bakery and another 10 in the suprmarket.
